# Feature Tags for Ad Analysis

Use these tags to categorize and track visual and audio elements in your ads. Each tag is detected automatically or can be manually added.

## Visual Features

### Content
| Tag | Meaning | Why It Matters |
|-----|---------|---|
| **shows_food** | Food or drink is visible | Food-focused ads often have higher engagement |
| **shows_people** | Human faces or bodies visible | Human presence increases emotional connection |
| **product_visible** | The advertised product is clearly shown | Product visibility drives conversion |
| **text_overlay** | Text or titles are overlaid on video | Text can distract but also clarify message |

### Aesthetics
| Tag | Meaning | Why It Matters |
|-----|---------|---|
| **color_dominant_warm** | Warm colors (orange, red, yellow) | Warm colors evoke energy and appetite |
| **color_dominant_cool** | Cool colors (blue, green, purple) | Cool colors feel calming and professional |
| **motion_heavy** | Fast cuts, pans, dynamic movement | Motion captures attention but can overwhelm |
| **animation** | Animated graphics or elements | Animation adds polish and engagement |

### Settings
| Tag | Meaning | Why It Matters |
|-----|---------|---|
| **outdoor_setting** | Scene is outdoors (nature, street, etc.) | Outdoor settings feel authentic |
| **indoor_setting** | Scene is indoors (home, office, store) | Indoor feels intimate or professional |

---

## Audio Features

### Speech
| Tag | Meaning | Why It Matters |
|-----|---------|---|
| **human_talking** | Human speech or dialogue present | Dialogue creates connection and clarity |
| **voiceover** | Professional voiceover narration | Voiceover adds authority and guidance |
| **silence** | Quiet or silent sections | Silence creates emphasis and pacing |

### Music & Sound
| Tag | Meaning | Why It Matters |
|-----|---------|---|
| **music** | Background music playing | Music sets mood and emotional tone |
| **sound_effects** | Sound effects or audio cues | Effects add drama and engagement |
| **upbeat_audio** | Fast-paced, energetic audio | Upbeat energy drives excitement |
| **calm_audio** | Slow, soothing audio | Calm feels professional and trustworthy |

---

## Overall Characteristics

| Tag | Meaning | Why It Matters |
|-----|---------|---|
| **fast_pacing** | Quick cuts, rapid editing | Pacing affects retention (faster = shorter attention span needed) |
| **emotional_appeal** | Emotional or inspirational messaging | Emotion drives sharing and remembering |
| **call_to_action** | Clear CTA (Call now, Visit, Buy, Download) | CTAs are critical for conversion |
| **humor** | Humorous or comedic content | Humor increases virality and recall |
| **aspirational** | Shows aspirational lifestyle or dreams | Aspiration drives desire and engagement |
| **problem_solution** | Shows problem, then offers solution | Problem-solution structure is proven persuasive |

## Correlation Analysis

Once you've tagged multiple ads, look for patterns:

**Example 1: High CTR Correlation**
```
Ads with high CTR all had:
✓ Human talking (builds connection)
✓ Clear call-to-action (directs action)
✓ Shows product (builds product awareness)
✗ Almost none had fast pacing (too overwhelming)
```

**Example 2: Low Conversion Despite High Views**
```
High-view ads lacked:
✗ Call-to-action (unclear what to do)
✗ Product visible (awareness without action)
✗ Emotional appeal (forgettable)
```

## How to Use This

1. **While analyzing an ad**, note which tags apply
2. **Record performance metrics** (CTR, ROAS, conversions, cost)
3. **Across 10+ ads**, group by high/medium/low performers
4. **Identify patterns** in feature combinations
5. **Update creative brief** based on top performers

Example brief evolution:
- **V1 (generic):** "Showcase the product, include music"
- **V2 (data-driven):** "Show product + human talking. Use upbeat music. Include clear CTA. Avoid fast cuts."
